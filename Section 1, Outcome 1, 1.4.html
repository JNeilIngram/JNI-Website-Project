1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects â€“ what causes digital projects to fail? Why?

Digital projects fail for a variety of reasons some of which are detailed below:-

  1. The project ignores the people who are meant to use it
  2. The project is too ambitious, trying to tackle all aspects of an existing system at once rather than breaking it down into manageable parts.
  3. "Mission Creep" - The goals of the project keep being changed by those who have ultimate responsibility for it (as opposed to those responsible for design and implementation)
  4. Technology - the project is too ambitious for the currently available technology
  5. Budget - the project has been given an unrealistic budget (either money or time)
  6. Infrastrucure - the existing infrastrucure may not be compatible with the preferred solution. For example a web based approach may be a perfect soluion for a particular situation, but is not implementable as the business is in an extremely rural area and has no broadband access.
