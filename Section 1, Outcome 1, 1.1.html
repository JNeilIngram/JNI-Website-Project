1.1 The purpose of IT in modern business

The most important thing to remember is:- IT is only ever a tool!

IT pervades all aspects of modern business life from CAD/CAM (Computer Aided Design/Computer Aided Manufacture) to the humble e-mail. It allows skilled people to do their jobs more effectively and also automates some tasks eliminating (hopefully) the chance of human error.

The principle concern when considering any IT system is that it actually seemlessly joins up business processes, whether they are simple or complex, internal (with colleagues) or external (customers/clients or suppliers), and does not become in itself an intrusive source of increased workload.

No one wants to hear "The computer says no!"
